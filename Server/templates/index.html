Agile Workstation Occupancy System

This system is designed in order to assist teaching and research staff in finding seating in the CB11 (UTS FEIT) Building.
It relies on a series of image sensors connected to Raspberry Pi throughout the building. Information relating to the occupancy status of individual desks is then relayed to this website, where the occupancy status of multiple seats can be viewed at once.
